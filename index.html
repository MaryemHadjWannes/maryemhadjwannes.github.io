About Me

I am a passionate Blockchain Developer with expertise in smart contract development, formal verification, and decentralized applications (DApps). I specialize in creating secure, reliable, and efficient blockchain solutions, particularly focusing on enhancing the reliability of smart contracts and integrating them into various industries, such as the olive oil supply chain and healthcare.

With over 2 years of experience in the blockchain field, I have worked extensively with Solidity, Ethereum, and various formal verification tools. My goal is to bridge the gap between complex blockchain technologies and practical real-world applications, ensuring robust, transparent, and scalable solutions for modern industries.

My Services

Smart Contract Development

I specialize in designing and developing secure and efficient smart contracts using Solidity, ensuring automation and trust in decentralized applications (DApps).

Blockchain Development

Security Audits
